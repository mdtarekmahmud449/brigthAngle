OUR STUDIO

We Develop Games with Creative Gameplay and Immersive Stories

BrightAngel-Media is a small independent studio, that is focusing on game development as our main priority. We offer a wide range of other services, ranging from website design and hosting to emotes and graphic design. From ourselves’s or the partners that we work with, in order to bring you the best experience that we can give you and which you will like very much.

We’re dedicated to providing you the best of services that we can offer, while also not breaking the bank. We do this with a focus on dependability.

We as a studio stand out, as we are a group of gamers who all bring our own experiences and gaming knowledge to the games that we make. Putting in our hearts, souls, and passions into the games we make, hoping you will love them as much as we do.
